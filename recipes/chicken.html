Moroccan Tomato Chicken

Flavorful marinated chicken thighs served alongside roasted vegetables, rice, and a cool mayo sauce.

Main Page

INGREDIENTS

Rice

  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 2 cups water

Vegetables

  • 1 large zucchini
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 2 to 3 carrots
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Chicken

  • 2 ½ lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • ¼ cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger

Mayo Sauce

  • ¼ cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 3 tbsp mayo
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp water

INSTRUCTIONS

Start with the rice

  1. Add 2 cups of water to a pot with salt, pepper, and butter or oil.
  2. Leave pot with lid off on a stove until water starts to boil.
  3. Once water is boiling, add the cup of rice.
  4. Turn stove to low, and put the lid on the pot. Leave for around 12 minutes.

Now the veggies

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F
  2. Wash and dice all of your vegetables.
  3. Add vegetables to a large bowl and toss with 1 tbsp of oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread out on a sheet pan and roast for 15-18 minutes, flipping around the 10 minute mark.

The chicken

  1. Cut the chicken thighs into one inch cubes.
  2. Mix all the other ingredients under the chicken section into one large bowl.
  3. Add the chicken cubes into the bowl and mix.
  4. Spread out the chicken into a large sheet pan, give pieces space so they may brown evenly.
  5. Turn your oven to broil and place the pan on the top rack.
  6. After 6-8 minutes, take the chicken out and toss it around.
  7. Place the chicken back for 4-5 minutes, watch it closely so it doesn't burn.

Sauce time

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl, season lightly with salt.

PLATING + NUTRITION

This recipe makes 5 servings. The nutrition info for 1 serving is as follows:

Calories: 570 | Protein: 43g | Carbohydrates: 46g | Fat: 23g
