Protein Pumpkin Muffins

This recipe results in about a dozen delicious pumpkin muffins.
Not only do these muffins taste great, they're also lower in calories and provide an exceptional amount of protein.

Main Page

DRY INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup of oat flour
  • ¼ cup of cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 scoop vanilla whey protein powder
  • 1 scoop vanilla casein protein powder

WET INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup liquid egg whites (or 2 eggs)
  • ⅔ cup full fat Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ¾ cup pumpkin puree
  • ¼ fourth cup maple syrup

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a large bowl, mix all the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the wet ingredients to the bowl, mix until a smooth batter is formed.
  3. Spray a 12 count muffin tin with oil.
  4. Pour batter evenly between wells.
  5. Bake at 350°F for 22 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool to room temperature before applying frosting.

NUTRITION

Muffin with no frosting: Calories: 91 | Protein: 7g | Carbohydrates: 11.7g | Fat: 1.8g

Muffin with 1 tbsp cream cheese frosting: Calories: 161 | Protein: 7g | Carbohydrates: 23.2g | Fat: 4.3g
